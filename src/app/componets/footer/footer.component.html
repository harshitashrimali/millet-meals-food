About

The Millet Meals came into existence as Vijaya Laxmi Subedar, Traditional home food enthusiast got inspired from her aging parents food habits. She realized the importance of healthy food habits as she learnt the secret of her parents’ healthy life, by adopting millet based foods they managed to avoid diseases like diabetes, B.P. etc. even at the age of 90’s.
